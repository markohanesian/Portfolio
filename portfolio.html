artGarden

A concept for a website with a unique bidding platform where users can post a contract and artists of all levels can bid on said contract. The idea behind artGarden is to give the “little guy” a chance at getting exposure instead of being pushed to the farthest back pages how they would on other freelancing gig sites. With contract bidding the top artists no longer will alway be promoted on the front page, giving new artists an equal opportunity to get selected.
